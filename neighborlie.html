Description

Neighborlie is a app that allows for free flowing communication among fellow tenants and their landlord. Landlords can create new buildings and assign their tenants to units accordingly. Once you are part of a building, you can see your community news feed, make posts and comments, and view the profiles of your fellow community members.

My classmate and I pair programed this app, and we were able to colaborate effectivily to meet the project deadline. While building the app and laying out the http routes, the main focuses kept in mind were to modularize code and remain RESTful. The app was built with Javascript, Node Js, Express, and MongoDB.
